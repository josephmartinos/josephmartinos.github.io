About

"Life is like riding a bicycle. To keep your balance, you must keep moving." — Albert Einstein

I am an accomplished Computer and Communication Engineer with over 20 years of experience at the intersection of technology and cybersecurity. Guided by Einstein's philosophy, I have deliberately kept moving — complementing a deep technical foundation with an Executive MBA from ESCP Europe Business School, giving me a holistic perspective that bridges security rigour with business strategy.

Over the years, I have developed an acute understanding of the risks that arise from vulnerabilities in IT systems, and this awareness shaped my transition into a focused cybersecurity career. Today, my expertise spans network security, cloud security, application security, risk management, and a wide range of industry frameworks including ISO 27001, NIST, CIS, PCI-DSS, and MITRE ATT&CK. I hold multiple industry certifications — CISSP, CCSP, GPCS, CCSK, CISM, and ISO 27005 — and I remain relentlessly committed to staying current in a field that never stands still.

Cybersecurity is more than a profession for me; it is a lifestyle. I take pride in being part of a community that works tirelessly to keep organizations safe in an increasingly digital world. If you are interested in collaborating to make that world a safer place, I look forward to connecting with you.
