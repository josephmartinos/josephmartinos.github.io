whoami

Cybersecurity Expert & Researcher

"Life is like riding a bicycle. To keep your balance, you must keep moving." — Albert Einstein

I am an accomplished Computer and Communication Engineer with over 20 years of experience at the intersection of technology and cybersecurity. Guided by Einstein's philosophy of constant forward motion, I have deliberately complemented a deep technical foundation with an Executive MBA from ESCP Europe Business School — giving me a rare perspective that bridges hands-on security engineering with strategic business thinking.

Today my work sits at the intersection of research, advisory, and the vendor landscape. I conduct and publish technical research on cloud security architecture and emerging security technologies. I work directly with CISOs and security architects to resolve complex technical challenges — whether that means defining a cloud security strategy, evaluating an architecture decision, or working through a difficult vendor selection. I also engage regularly with technology vendors to assess their latest developments and roadmaps. My clients and audiences are global, spanning Fortune 500 enterprises, government agencies, and public sector organizations across multiple industries and geographies.

My expertise spans cloud security architecture, network security, application security, risk management, Zero Trust, SASE, and security frameworks including ISO 27001, NIST CSF, CIS Controls, PCI-DSS, and MITRE ATT&CK. Whether it is helping a CISO define their cloud security strategy, evaluating vendors, designing a security reference architecture, or guiding a team through a complex multi-cloud implementation — I bring both the analytical rigour and the practical experience to get it done. I hold certifications including CISSP, CCSP, GPCS, CCSK, CISM, and ISO 27005.

I also teach network security at Polytechnique Montréal and serve as a Community Champion at Rogers Cybersecure Catalyst. Cybersecurity is more than a profession for me — it is a lifestyle and a responsibility. If you are looking for someone who can both think at the architecture level and deliver at the implementation level, I look forward to connecting.

Manager, Cloud Security
